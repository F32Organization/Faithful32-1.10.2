Texture Artists:

In all _side textures the sides are laid out in the order west, north, east, south
In all _top_bottom textures the quad on the left is the top, the one on the right is the bottom
In gobletStand_sides, the upper half is the west and north sides, left to right. The east and south sides are the bottom half.
In kelkWall, the two large quads are the large faces, the strips to the left or right are the small faces.
    The strips on top of the large quads are the top/bottom textures of the N/S walls of the cup
    The vertical strips on the right are the top/bottom textures of W/E walls of the cup
